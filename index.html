Women's Soccer Scouting - Interactive Visualization

How to Use

  • Scene 1: Overview - Nationalities of Top 100 Players:
    • Hover over a bar to view the nationality name in a tooltip.
    • Click on a bar to drill down to Scene 2 and explore leagues in the selected nationality.
  • Scene 2: Drill Down - Leagues in Selected Nationality:
    • Hover over a bar to view the league name in a tooltip.
    • Click on a bar to drill down to Scene 3 and explore physical attributes of players in the selected league and nationality.
  • Scene 3: Conclusion - Physical Attributes of Players in Selected League and Nationality:
    • Explore the scatter plot to analyze the correlation between player height (x-axis) and weight (y-axis).

Use the drill-down functionality to gain deeper insights and find metrics to look for in different leagues.

This data represents the top 100 women players of all time according to FIFA 23. Use this narrative visualization to discover which nations and leagues produce the best players and explore the physical attributes of those players. This tool can help you identify the key metrics to look for in different leagues.
